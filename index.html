Our Story

Where Every Evening
Becomes Extraordinary

Since 2010, Jyde Lounge has defined the pinnacle of Lagos nightlife — a sanctuary where impeccable cuisine, expertly crafted cocktails, and an electric atmosphere converge. Our commitment is unwavering: to deliver experiences that linger in memory long after the night ends.

Nestled in the heart of Surulere, we have hosted Lagos's finest — celebrating milestones, forging connections, and creating moments that define a generation.
